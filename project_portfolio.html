About Kate

Born and raised in Pittsburgh, Pennslyvania.
Schooled in Communication Design at Carnegie Mellon University.

As a visual learner I work to create conceptually sound and visually cohesive systems. As an analytical thinker I work to identify the problem, potential responses, possible impacts, and implications of my designs. And as a researcher I search for patterns, outliers, and shared stories to inform users' needs.
