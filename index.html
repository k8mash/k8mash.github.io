Hi!

I am a designer and plant enthusiast living in San Francisco, CA.

I am consistently curious, seeking opportunities to learn, gain new skills and solve complex problems. It's safe to say I am never without a side project.

Currently I work as an Interaction Designer at Method, designing products and experiences for the agriculture and education industries. Previously, I was an amatuer farm hand in the foothills of the Sierra Nevadas, a Product Designer at IBM in Austin, TX and a communication design student at Carnegie Mellon University in Pittsburgh, PA.

Please reference my experience below and contact me by email for my most recent work samples.
