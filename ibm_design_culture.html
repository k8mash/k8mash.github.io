IBM Austin Studios Wayfinding Update

As IBM Studios Austin expanded in both space and employees, there was a need to improve the wayfinding system. ___ percent of new designers had difficulty orienting themselves and after a year, ____ percent ________. To improve the experience and ease of use for our designers and visitors, I conducted research and lead the redesign of a signage system, that build off current regions and landmarks in the studio to unite the multiple floors of the studio. Below are the directional, stairwell, and room signs. Illustrations were included to provide texture and build off the IBM heritage that was carried throughout the studio and room naming system.
