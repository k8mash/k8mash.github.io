About Kate

I believe that design can be a unifier.

It can connect, translate, simplify, encode and reveal information to its audiences in differenct mediums and on different levels. I believe design must come from deep understanding and intent. As a communication designer, I believe there is great responsibility to translate intention and meaning into a cohesive message, system or experience.

I believe that design should serve.

It can not exist in a vacuum and must always have an environment, purpose, user and intent. As an experience designer I believe that design is built in systems and through the understanding of the user, possible implications and impacts of any design response.

Originally from Pittsburgh, PA, I spent the last two years living and working in Austin, TX. Currently I am in Northern California and looking for full-time, part-time, and contractor work in the US and Europe. Please take a look at my resume and get in touch!
