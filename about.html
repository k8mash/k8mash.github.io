About Kate

I believe that design can be a unifier.

It can connect, translate, simplify, encode and reveal information to its audiences in different mediums and on different levels. As a communication designer, I believe there is great responsibility to translate intention and meaning into a cohesive message, system or experience.

I believe that design should serve.

It can not exist in a vacuum and must always have an environment, purpose, user and intent. As an experience designer I believe that design is built in systems and through the understanding of the user, possible implications and impacts of any design response.

Originally from Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, I spent the last two years living and working in Austin, Texas. Currently I am living in the foothills of the Sierra Nevadas, learning about gardening, food preservation and winemaking on a small ten acre farm. In addition to design and farming, I enjoy working with my hands to cook, crochet or draw, and getting outside to hike, run or do a little yoga.

I am actively looking for full-time, part-time and/or contractor work in the United States and Europe.
