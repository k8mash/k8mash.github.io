About Kate

As a visual learner I gain insight through iteration, to deliver conceptually sound and visually cohesive systems. As an analytical thinker I identify the problem(s) and systems, potential responses, possible impacts, and implications of my designs. And as a researcher I search for patterns, outliers, and shared stories to inform users' needs. I am excited by the opportunities design and systems thinking, and technology have in radically shaping a more connected, responsible, and sustainable future.
